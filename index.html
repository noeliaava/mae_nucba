Meet & English

Do you want to speak fluent English with us?

- ABOUT US -

Meet & English is formed by a group of students and professionals in the IT industry who chat online and also meet to talk in English. We hold meetings for which we prepare different assingments. We have fun and learn a lot in the way.

Our mission is to help each other to practice, learn and acknowledge the English language through chatting and playing games.

We encourage our members to share who they are, not what they know. Our four core values are integrity, responsibility, respect and partnership.

We plan to become an example for others to make the same decision on joining people together with a common purpose: learn, share and enjoy.
